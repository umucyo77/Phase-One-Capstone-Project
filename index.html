About Book Explorer

Welcome to Book Explorer!

Discover and explore thousands of books from the OpenLibrary database. Whether you're looking for classics, contemporary fiction, or non-fiction, our app makes it easy to search, explore, and keep track of your favorite books.

Features:

  • Search: Find books by title, author, or genre using our powerful search engine.
  • Book Details: Click "View" on any book to see detailed information and cover images.
  • Favorites: Add books to your favorites list with one click. Your favorites are saved locally.
  • Responsive Design: Enjoy a seamless experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

How to Use:

  1. Search for books by title in the search bar.
  2. Click "View" to see book details, or "Add" to save to your favorites.
  3. Visit the Favorites page to view all your saved books.
  4. Remove books from favorites anytime.

Powered by OpenLibrary, an open source project by the Internet Archive.
